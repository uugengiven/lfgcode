Gaming

Local Servers

Play on our local servers in Minecraft, Starbound and others. We keep player stats and host whatever games are requested and possible to host. Seriously, if you want something to be hosted so you can play multiplayer, ask us and we'll probably install it that day.

    • Classes

    We don't just play at LFG, we also learn and build. Here are some of the classes we are offering, most of them free. We will be putting up our class schedule as soon as possible but if you have a class idea or want to take one of our classes, let us know now and we will make sure to let you know when it is ready.

    Build Your Own Console

    Learn how to build your own console using a Raspberry Pi. This class costs ~$100 in materials but at the end, you take home your own retro console that has multiple games installed. You will learn how to install the OS, connect to your console, copy files over and configure emulators and controls. Take home copies of Doom and Duke Nukem 3D with your console!

    Mods

    Using the Starcraft 2 or Dota 2 tools, learn how to build maps and mods then play your mods with other people. Learn how to start using content creation tools, triggers and different elements to build out your own perfect map or mod.

    Servers

    Learn how to build and maintain your own Minecraft server using Linux and Docker. Learn how to change settings, add mods and set up your server to create your own version of Minecraft.
